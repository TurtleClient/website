FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove the cape?

You can permanently disable the capes feature by navigating to the client's files -> Textures -> And deleting cosmetics directory.

How to download the client using Linkvertise?

Watch this YouTube video.

Where can I find a list of all features that turtle client has?

You can go to Settings -> Turtle Client to find a list of all the features.

Can i get hacked using this client!??!?!?!

Wtf? no. It's only a texture pack meaning it can only modify your Minecraft textures and nothing else.

Can I use this client on mobile?

Turtle client is available for XBOX, Mobile, PS4 and Windows 10 (Minecraft: Bedrock Edition)

My Armor HUD and etc. are not aligned! They're mixing in with the hotbar!

Go to settings -> video -> change your GUI Scale setting.

All the cool settings called "Atmospherics" and stuff in the debug menu are not working!

If you're NOT on an official Mojang Developer account, these settings will not work. Including the Debug settings.

Can I modify turtle client?

As long as it is for personal use, yes. Meaning you cannot distribute this as your own client or a modified version of our client to the public.
